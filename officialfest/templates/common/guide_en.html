1. The story

Igor is a snowman who lives in the far away and cold land of Hammerfest. This beautiful country of green hills and snow-covered mountains was once shattered by the evil powers of Tuber the Sorcerer. The fruits were cursed and kidnapped and Igor found himself sad and alone without his friends.

The intrepid Igor decided to go and rescue his friends the Fruits and to destroy the evil sorcerer's plan...

2. Playing a game (snowflakes)

When you create an account, you have 5 free games. Each day you'll have a new free game (which you cannot accumulate).

You can buy more games in the Shop. These games can be accumulated and are kept in your account.

Do you want to win games and extra lives? Take a look at the Quests section for further information.

3. Playing: Choosing a Game mode

To start Playing , click on the big icon of Igor in the ice cube, situated on the left side of the site. Select a Game mode and click on "Start game".

There are several game modes:

  • The main game mode is the Adventure mode . In the Adventure mode you'll find quests, unblock new game modes, obtain points to climb up the pyramid, etc. Each game costs 1 .
  • The Learning mode is highly recommended for beginners. With this mode you learn the basic techniques before playing the Adventure mode. The Learning mode is free and unlimited.
  • About the other Game modes , you have to unblock and discover them on your own!

4. The fridge

The Fridge is the place where Igor keeps his collection of items. The items can be obtained throughout the game in the Adventure mode.. Usually you need to obtain 10 times the item before being allowed to keep it in the Fridge.

The Fridge is of no practical use in the game. Its only function is for keeping souvenirs of your adventures in the caverns.

5. The Pyramid (ranking)

The Pyramid is a weekly league which includes every player who has played in the Adventure mode.

At the end of each game in the Adventure mode, the number of points (score) obtained is saved in the Pyramid ranking.

Each player starts at the lowest Pyramid level: level 4.

Every Tuesday at midnight, the ranking is brought back to zero and the best players of each level go up a level.

The best player of level 1 wins a place in the Hall of Fame ! Thanks to this title, he/she will be a living legend and will be able to leave a message in the Great Book of the Heroes.

Pay attention because only the score is brought down to zero. Your possessions, bought games and quests are kept up.

6. The quests

In the Quest page you have a briefing of all the started Quests, finished and in progress.

Throughout your games in the Adventure mode, you'll find several items with different effects. Sometimes they'll only give you points, but other times they'll have devastating powers over your enemies or over you.

Most of these items are part of long Quests.

A Quest consists of accumulating a series of items. Each time you find a Quest item, it will be validated and its effects will be permanently applied to all your future games!

These effects are different and sometimes when you finish a Quest you can obtain:

  • Extra lives
  • Extra games
  • New quests available for exploration
  • New game modes
  • More lives each time you start a game in the Adventure mode
  • New skills
  • New games each time you make a purchase in the shop
  • And plenty of other things!

7. Game options

At the bottom of the Game menu, click on the link to change some options during the game:

  • High quality graphics. To get a better game performance, disable the box.
  • Frequent vibrations. If you think the vibrations are slowing down the game, disable this box.
  • Sound. Sound effects (jumps, explosions, etc.) are activated if you enable this box.
  • Music. Enable this box to listen to the music of the game during your adventure.
  • Volume. Turn up or down the volume with this option.

8. The forum

Are you lost in the dark caverns of Hammerfest? Are you wondering what the giant crystal, which falls down sometimes, is for? Ask all your questions in the forum! Administrators, moderators and players will help you to solve them.

9. Technical support
